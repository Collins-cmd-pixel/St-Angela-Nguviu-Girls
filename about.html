Principal's Message

Welcome to Nyangwa Boys High School, a beacon of excellence along the Embu-Kiritiri road near Kiritiri market. Since our establishment, we have remained committed to holistic education that shapes character, intellect, and leadership.

We take a boy and give you back a Man. Our dedicated staff nurture young minds with discipline, determination, and dedication, preparing them to become responsible citizens and future leaders of our nation.

Join us in this noble journey of transformation.

— Mr. Silas Njeru, Chief Principal

Our History

Nyangwa Boys High School, fondly known as the Typhoons, was founded in the late 1960s as a premier boys' boarding institution in Embu County. Over the decades, we have consistently produced top KCSE performers, national debate champions, and rugby stars while instilling values of hard work, integrity, and service.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To transform young boys into disciplined, knowledgeable, honorable, and compassionate men ready for leadership roles in society through quality CBC education and character development.

Vision

To be a leading centre of academic excellence, producing well-rounded gentlemen who serve with dedication and lead with integrity.

Core Values

Determination • Dedication • Discipline • Integrity • Excellence • Service
