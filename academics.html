Competency-Based Education (CBE)

Nyangwa Boys High School fully embraces the Competency-Based Education (CBE) for Senior Secondary levels. We focus on developing 7 core competencies: communication & collaboration, critical thinking & problem solving, creativity & imagination, citizenship, digital literacy, learning to learn, and self-efficacy. Our approach ensures boys are equipped for real-world success while maintaining strong academic performance.
