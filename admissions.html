Admission Requirements

  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Latest School Report
  • Copy of KCPE Result Slip
  • Passport-size photos (4)
  • Copy of Parent/Guardian ID
  • Medical Report/Immunization Records
  • Entrance Exam Fee Receipt - KSh 1,000 (For Transfers)
  • For transfers: Clearance letter from previous school
